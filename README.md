# Sales Performance Dashboard Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing company sales performance using Microsoft Excel dashboards. The goal of the project is to identify trends in revenue, product performance, regional sales, customer behavior, sales channels, and payment methods to support data-driven business decision-making.

---

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze company sales performance using Microsoft Excel dashboards and identify business trends related to revenue, products, regions, sales channels, customer types, and payment methods.

---

## Dataset Overview
The dataset contains sales transaction details including:

- Sales Amount
- Orders
- Product IDs
- Product Categories
- Sales Regions
- Sales Channels
- Quantity Sold
- Sales Representatives
- Customer Types
- Payment Methods

The project was developed using Microsoft Excel Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Dashboard visualization techniques.

## Key Business Insights
- The North region generated the highest sales revenue.
- Product 1009 was identified as the top-performing product.
- Returning customers contributed more revenue compared to new customers.
- Sales representatives showed varying levels of performance.
- Payment methods were distributed almost evenly across customers.
- Certain product categories contributed significantly more revenue than others.
- Customer purchasing behavior differed across sales channels.

---

## Statistical Validation

### Hypothesis Testing

### Null Hypothesis (H₀)
There is no significant difference in sales performance between regions.

### Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)
There is a significant difference in sales performance between regions.

A basic statistical comparison was performed to evaluate sales variation across regions. The findings suggest that regional sales performance differs, indicating the importance of region-specific business strategies.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
- Improve strategies in underperforming regions.
- Reward top-performing sales representatives.
- Develop retention strategies for returning customers.
- Optimize payment options for better customer convenience.
- Maintain inventory for high-demand products and categories.

## Conclusion
This project successfully transformed raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Excel dashboards and visualization techniques. The analysis supports better decision-making related to sales strategy, customer behavior, product management, and regional performance optimization.
