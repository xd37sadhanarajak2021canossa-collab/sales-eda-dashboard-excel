# Sales Performance Dashboard Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing company sales performance using Microsoft Excel dashboards. The goal of the project is to identify trends in revenue, orders, product performance, sales regions, and sales channels to support better business decision-making.

---

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales data and transform raw business information into meaningful insights through interactive dashboard visualizations.

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset contains:
- Sales Amount
- Total Orders
- Product IDs
- Product Categories
- Sales Regions
- Sales Channels
- Quantity Sold

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Dashboard Design
- Data Visualization
- Basic Statistical Validation

---

## Analysis Performed
The following analysis was performed:
- Total Revenue Analysis
- Total Orders Analysis
- Average Sales Calculation
- Sales by Region
- Top Performing Products
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Sales Channel

---

## Dashboard Features
The dashboard includes:
- KPI Cards
- Regional Sales Comparison
- Product Performance Analysis
- Category-wise Sales Analysis
- Sales Channel Visualization

---

## Key Insights
- The North region generated the highest sales revenue.
- Product 1009 was identified as the top-performing product.
- Sales performance varied across different regions.
- Certain product categories contributed more significantly to revenue.
- Sales channels showed different customer purchasing behaviors.

---

## Statistical Validation

### Hypothesis Testing

**Null Hypothesis (H₀):**
There is no significant difference in sales performance between regions.

**Alternative Hypothesis (H₁):**
There is a significant difference in sales performance between regions.

A basic statistical comparison was conducted to evaluate regional sales variation. The findings suggest that sales performance differs across regions, indicating the need for region-specific business strategies.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing regions.
- Improve strategies for underperforming regions.
- Maintain inventory for high-demand products.
- Focus promotions on top-performing categories.
- Optimize sales channels based on customer behavior.

---

## Conclusion
This project successfully transformed sales data into actionable business insights through dashboard visualization and storytelling. The analysis can help businesses improve decision-making related to sales strategy, product management, and regional performance optimization.
